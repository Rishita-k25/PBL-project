💡 Resume Tips

📏 Keep your resume to 1–2 pages maximum for entry-level roles.
🎯 Tailor your resume for each job. Match keywords from the job description.
💡 Use action verbs: Built, Designed, Implemented, Led, Achieved.
📊 Quantify achievements: "Reduced load time by 40%" beats vague statements.
🔤 Use a clean, ATS-friendly font like Arial, Calibri, or Times New Roman.
✅ Proofread carefully. Even small grammar errors can cost you an interview.
